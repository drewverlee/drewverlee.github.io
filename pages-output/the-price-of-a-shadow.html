run, boy run

under twilight gaze
your flight a flickering haze

run, boy run

hush, quietly they come
faint as flickerlight foxfire
giving up their little souls

run, boy run

over sunny meadow
your breath like pleading piper
your canter like tabor beats

away, away, away...

why won't you stay?
you have so much left to play
and you can't outrun your shadow
